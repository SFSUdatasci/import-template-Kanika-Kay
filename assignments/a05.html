Introduction

Discussion

The graph shows the total number of visits to delta restoration. It separates the data by visitor type and what location it is. The colors correspond to the types of visitors. This shows all of the data in one central graph. The map shows the locations that the samples were collected from. The red dots correspond to locations that were sampled. The text is for the cities that are nearby. The map is also interactive. You can zoom in and click on the dots for the location names.
